She is currently pursuing PhD of Electronics Science and Technology from 2019 at the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in China and Research Affiliate at State Key Laboratory of Information Photonics and Optical Communications. I graduated with a Master in Communications Engineering from Soochow University in June 2019.

Her research interests include quantum key distribution networking and optical networking.

Up to now, she has published 4 journal papers and 9 conference papers. She has published 2 chapters in different English books. She has granted 5 China patents and submitted 3 China patents. She gained the Gold Medal at Geneva International Exhibition of Inventions in 2021. She also participated in many international standards works, and she has submitted 24 drafts to ITU-T SG13. She has led a standard project-Supplement 70 to ITU-T Y.3800-series (ex Y.QKDN-mla) named “Quantum Key Distribution Networks - Applications of Machine Learning” and a standard recommendation project- Recommendation Y.QKDN-ml-fra named “Quantum key distribution networks - functional requirements and architecture for machine learning”. Currently, she is leading a standard recommendation project- Recommendation Y.QKDN-amc named “Quantum key distribution networks - Requirements and architectural model for autonomic management and control”.
